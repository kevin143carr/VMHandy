# VMHandy Editing Script

## Editing Direction

Format: fast cuts, punchy zooms, whoosh transitions, bold text cards.

Sequence order:

1. Open with chaotic desktop/Finder montage.
2. Use `0.5-1.0` second cuts for the first `10` seconds.
3. Add sharp record-scratch or hit before `VMHandy!`
4. Switch visual tone from chaos to control once app appears.
5. Highlight only one action per shot:
   `Provider` -> `Copy To Local` -> `Register` -> `Launch VM`
6. Use large centered captions for action verbs.
7. For the side-by-side section, split screen:
   left `Parallels`, right `VMware Fusion`
8. During `Click. Click. Click. Done.` sync each word to a distinct UI action.
9. End with slower pacing for the final `5` seconds.
10. Hold end card at least `2` seconds.

## Suggested Sound Design

- chaotic clicks and drive mount sounds in opener
- impact hit on `VMHandy!`
- subtle UI clicks on each action
- rising whoosh into launch
- clean resolved music sting on final card
